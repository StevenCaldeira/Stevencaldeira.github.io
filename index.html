Project Management – USC Housing Application
Introduction:

The USC Housing App project addresses the pressing need for a streamlined, centralized platform for USC students to manage their housing-related requirements. With the challenges of locating reliable housing reviews, finding compatible roommates, and coordinating housing-associated transactions, USC students face a complex, time-consuming, and often fragmented process. The USC Housing App is designed to simplify and enhance the student housing experience by consolidating essential features such as housing reviews, roommate matching, and a secure marketplace into one accessible application. Built using technologies like Flutter for compatibility with both iOS and Android, the app will integrate with USC’s Single Sign-On (SSO) system, ensuring a safe and seamless user experience. This initiative not only aims to provide convenience but also promotes sustainability by encouraging the reuse of goods within the student community. Additionally, the app will generate revenue through in-app advertisements and featured listings, creating a sustainable financial model for USC.

Problem Statement:
  • USC students currently encounter multiple challenges in managing their housing needs.
  • The lack of a unified platform for housing search, roommate finding, and marketplace transactions leads to inefficiencies, scattered information, and sometimes unreliable resources.
  • Existing alternatives, such as social media groups or third-party housing websites, do not provide the same level of security, student-focused features, or verified information.
  • These platforms lack integration with USC’s infrastructure, which would enhance security and usability.
  • The USC Housing App addresses these challenges by providing a single, student-centered solution that offers verified housing options, compatibility-based roommate matching, and a trusted marketplace for goods.
  • By implementing this app, USC aims to enhance student experience, streamline housing processes, and contribute to sustainable practices through product reuse.
